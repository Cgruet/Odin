Chicken Casserole

Description

A creamy, comforting dish that has the perfect creamy-crunch ratio. It's great way to use up a leftover rotisserie chicken. The oven does all the work baking this casserole, so you can spend more time with your family. It's great way to use up leftover chicken!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Cream cheese
  • Cottage cheese
  • Pepper Jack cheese
  • Sour Cream
  • Green onions
  • Cream of chicken soup
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Ritz crackers
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Combine cream cheese, cottage cheese, sour cream, garlic powder, onion powder and cream of chicken soup in mixing bowl.
  2. Stir in pepper jack cheese and green onions and cooked chicken until combined.
  3. Mix together melted butter and ritz crackers.
  4. Top chicken mixture with crackers and bake until golden brown.
